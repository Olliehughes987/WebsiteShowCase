What is Mental Wellbeing?

Mental wellbeing is how we feel inside, its how we percieve happiness in our day to day lives. However, a large number of people suffer with poor mental health. It is important to state that this is not the fault of the person with poor mental health, as it can be caused by a wide range of things.

Fortunately, the conversation regarding mental health has become a lot more open in the past years, meaning more and more people are feeling comfortable about getting the help they need.

Depression

Depression is a constant feeling of sadness and loss of interest, which stops you doing your normal activities. This can come in many forms, such as a lack of energy or motivation to achieve daily tasks. Depression can also affect people's social lives as they may feel unable to participate in group activities.
