Physical Wellbeing

Physical Wellbeing is as important as Mental to ensure that you are as healthy as possible. It includes lifestyle behavior choices to ensure health, avoid preventable diseases and conditions, and to live in a balanced state of body, mind, and spirit. It is important to manage any negative behaviours that arise to maintain happiness and comfort within your life.

Diet

One of the core ways to maintain your physical wellbeing is to ensure that you are healthy. This can be achieved through diet and exercise. One of the main ways is to ensure that your calorie intake is at or below your daily maintenence level.

You can find out your daily recommended carlorie intake by either using your bmr, which can be found out online or at your local gp.
