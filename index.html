Welcome to the website!

This website will help you find useful information about mental wellbeing, physical wellbeing, and ways of accessing support available. It was created as part of my university project to learn front end web development.

What is wellbeing?

Wellbeing is defined as The state of being comfortable, healthy, or happy.

1 in 4 people will experience a mental health problem of some kind each year in England. Together, we can work to decrease this number by providing the support and knowledge needed for people to seek help.

One of the problems is the stigma attatched to reaching out for help and support, this leads to many young adults being too afraid to ask for the help they need.

Important notes

This website will help guide you through wellbeing and the support available. However, this website contains no legal or health advice and asking your doctor for advice is crucial if you believe you suffer from poor wellbeing.
