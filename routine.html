Routine Planner

This page will allow you to create a routine for you to follow, you can add or remove the text boxes to set the length of the routine to whatever is suitable for you. However, there is a max limit of 8 boxes due to technical restrictions.

How to use:

Click the "+" button until you have the desired amount of text boxes for your routine. You can click on the text boxes to be able to type inside of them, and click "save" once you are done. If you wish to load your previous routine, then press the "load" button.
